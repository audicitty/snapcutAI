Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear rules about cancellations, refunds, and disputes — no fine print.

📅 Effective: 24 May 2026 🔄 Last updated: 24 May 2026

Short version: Credits are refundable within 7 days if unused. Pro subscriptions can be cancelled anytime and are refundable within 3 days if no cutouts were made. To request a refund, email us at divyanshubansal323@gmail.com.

1. Scope

This policy applies to all purchases made on SnapCut — including the Pro subscription plan and credit packs — processed via Razorpay. By completing a purchase, you agree to this policy.

2. Credit Packs (One-Time Purchase)

2.1 Refund Window

You may request a full refund for a credit pack purchase within 7 calendar days of the date of purchase, provided no credits have been used.

2.2 After Credits Are Used

Once any credits from a pack have been consumed (i.e., at least one background removal has been processed), the remaining credits in that pack are non-refundable.

2.3 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds are not provided. If even one credit has been used, the entire pack is considered consumed for refund purposes.

2.4 Credits Never Expire

Unused credits do not expire. They remain available in your account indefinitely.

3. Pro Subscription (Monthly / Yearly)

3.1 Cancellation

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Upon cancellation:

  • Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period.
  • You will not be charged for the next billing cycle.
  • No partial-period refunds are issued for the remaining days of the current billing period.

3.2 Refund Within 3 Days of Purchase

If you have subscribed to Pro and have not processed any cutouts within 3 calendar days of the initial purchase date, you are eligible for a full refund of that subscription payment. Contact us within this window to claim it.

3.3 After Cutouts Are Processed

Once you have used the Pro service to process one or more images, the subscription payment for that billing period is non-refundable.

3.4 Yearly Plan

For yearly plans, the same 3-day / zero-cutout rule applies. After the window, no refund is issued for the yearly payment, but you retain Pro access for the full year.

4. Free Plan

The Free plan involves no payment. There is nothing to refund or cancel. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

5. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please email us at divyanshubansal323@gmail.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Razorpay payment ID (found in your payment confirmation email)
  • Reason for refund request
  • The plan or credit pack you purchased

We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and process eligible refunds within 7–10 business days. Refunds are credited to the original payment method (card, UPI, wallet, etc.).

6. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Credits partially or fully used
  • Pro subscription where cutouts have been processed
  • Requests made after the applicable refund window (7 days for credits, 3 days for Pro)
  • Purchases made under a promotional or discounted price where terms stated "non-refundable"
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms & Conditions

7. Chargebacks & Disputes

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. Unauthorised chargebacks may result in account suspension. If you have a legitimate dispute, we are committed to resolving it fairly and promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the website. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase governs that transaction.
