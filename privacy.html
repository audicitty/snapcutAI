Privacy Policy

We keep things simple: your images stay yours and your data stays safe.

📅 Effective: 24 May 2026 🔄 Last updated: 24 May 2026 🏢 SnapCut, Bangalore, India

Short version: We collect only what we need to operate the service. Your uploaded photos are processed and deleted — we never store originals. Payments go through Razorpay; we never see your card details.

1. Who We Are

SnapCut ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered image background removal service operated by Divyanshu Bansal, trading as SnapCut, based in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.

Contact: divyanshubansal323@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. This is used to identify your account and send you transactional emails (payment receipts, usage alerts).

2.2 Images You Upload

When you upload an image for background removal:

  • Your original image is sent to our AI processing pipeline.
  • The processed (background-removed) image is temporarily stored on Cloudinary to generate a download link.
  • Original uploaded images are not permanently stored by us and are deleted after processing.
  • Processed result images on Cloudinary may be retained for up to 24 hours to allow you to download them, after which they are automatically purged.
  • We do not use your images to train AI models.

2.3 Payment Information

All payments are processed by Razorpay (PCI DSS Level 1 compliant). We never receive, store, or have access to your full card number, CVV, or bank credentials. We only receive a payment confirmation ID from Razorpay upon successful payment.

2.4 Usage Data

We may collect basic usage data such as the number of cutouts processed, plan type, and browser type. This helps us improve the service and is not linked to your individual images.

2.5 Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to store your session, credit balance, and history of processed image URLs. This data lives only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process your image background removal requests
  • To facilitate and confirm payments via Razorpay
  • To send transaction and account-related emails
  • To detect and prevent abuse or fraud
  • To improve our AI model performance (using aggregated, anonymised metrics only — never your actual images)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate SnapCut:

  • Razorpay — payment processing. Governed by Razorpay's own privacy policy.
  • Cloudinary — temporary storage of processed images for download. Governed by Cloudinary's privacy policy.
  • n8n — workflow automation for image processing. Images are passed through n8n solely for processing and are not retained.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure upon request.
  • Original uploaded images: Not stored; deleted immediately after processing.
  • Processed result images: Deleted from Cloudinary within 24 hours.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at divyanshubansal323@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

8. Security
