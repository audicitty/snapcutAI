Shipping & Delivery Policy

SnapCut is a 100% digital service. There is no physical product or shipping involved.

📅 Effective: 24 May 2026 🔄 Last updated: 24 May 2026

Short version: SnapCut is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) product. All deliverables are digital — processed images and account credits. Delivery is instant, online, and worldwide. No physical goods are shipped.

1. Nature of the Service

SnapCut provides AI-powered background removal as a digital service accessible via our website. There are no physical goods, CDs, USB drives, or any tangible product associated with any purchase on SnapCut.

The "deliverables" you receive are:

  • Access to SnapCut's online background removal tool
  • Processed, background-removed PNG image files available for download
  • Credits added to your account (for credit pack purchases)
  • Upgraded plan features (for Pro subscription purchases)

2. Delivery of Digital Service

2.1 Credit Packs

Upon successful payment confirmation from Razorpay, credits are added to your SnapCut account immediately (typically within seconds). You can start using the credits to process images right away.

2.2 Pro Subscription

Upon successful subscription payment, your account is upgraded to Pro status instantly. All Pro features (unlimited cutouts, higher resolution, priority processing) become available immediately.

2.3 Processed Images

After you upload an image and click "Remove Background", the processed PNG is ready for download within approximately 2–5 seconds. The result is available directly in your browser and in your History tab.

3. How Delivery Works — Step by Step

  • 1
    You complete a payment via Razorpay (card, UPI, net banking, or wallet).
  • 2
    Razorpay sends us a payment confirmation in real time.
  • 3
    Your account is credited or upgraded instantly — no manual steps required.
  • 4
    You upload your image, our AI processes it, and a transparent PNG is ready for download within seconds.
  • 5
    You download the PNG directly to your device. Your processed history is saved locally in your browser.

4. Availability

SnapCut is a globally accessible online service. There are no geographic restrictions on using the service. An internet connection is required to access SnapCut and process images.

We target 99.5% uptime. In the event of scheduled maintenance or unexpected downtime, we will communicate via the website. If you are unable to use your credits or Pro features due to downtime lasting more than 24 hours, contact us for an appropriate remedy.

5. Failed or Delayed Delivery

In rare cases, credit additions or plan upgrades may be delayed due to:

  • Payment processor delays (Razorpay webhook latency)
  • Server errors on our end
  • Browser or network issues on your end

To be unambiguous: SnapCut does not ship any physical product to any address. Providing a shipping address is not required and is not requested at any point. All services are rendered electronically via the internet.
